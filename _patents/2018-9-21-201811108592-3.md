核心方法
-----
根据问题及答案，筛选候选模板集，并通过机器学习算法对候选集排序，使用最合适的模板封装答案。  

摘要
-----
本申请提供的一种数据处理方法、服务器及电子设备，在获得问题数据以及问题数据所对应的答案信息基础上，基于问题数据所对应的问题模型确定所述答案信息所对应的答案模型，并进而基于所述答案模型生成问题数据对应的至少一个候选答案语句，由于针对问题数据，生成了符合其答案模型的至少一个候选答案语句，从而为答案语句的确定提供了语言表述形式(语句形式)方面的选择空间，能够支持智能问答系统从中选择出更加贴近人类自然语言的答案语句，进而可有效提升智能问答系统在语言表述形式方面的答案质量。    

Abstract
----
the invention provides a data processing method,a server and an electronic device. on the basis of obtaining the question data and the answer information corresponding to the question data, determining an answer model corresponding to the answer information based on the question model corresponding to the question data, and further generating at least one candidate answer statement correspondingto the question data based on the answer model, in accordance with that problem data, generating at least one candidate answer statement that conforms to its answer model, Thus, the choice space of language expression forms (sentence forms) is provided for the determination of answer sentences, the intelligent question answering system can be supported to select the answer sentences which are closer to human natural language, and the answer quality of the intelligent question answering system in language expression forms can be effectively improved.
