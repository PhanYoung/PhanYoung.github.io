核心方法
-----
使用机器学习标注算法结构化解析实体名称，并基于字符相似、行业知识特征进行实体对齐。  

摘要
-----
本发明公开了一种实体匹配方法、装置及电子设备，该方法包括：获取待匹配的实体名称；从包含多个标准实体名称的标准名称库中，搜索出与实体名称的匹配度较高的多个候选实体名称；确定实体名称与候选实体名称分别在至少一个预设维度上的相似性；综合实体名称与候选实体名称在至少一个预设维度上的相似性，从多个候选实体名称中，确定出与实体名称在至少一个预设维度上的综合相似性最高的候选实体名称；将综合相似性最高的候选实体名称确定为实体名称对应的标准实体名称。本申请的方案可以增大实体名称匹配所适用的情况，提高实体名称匹配的精准度，并减少人力资源消耗。    

Abstract
----
The invention discloses an entity matching method and apparatus, and an electronic device. The method comprises the steps of obtaining an entity name to be matched; Searching a plurality of candidateentity names with higher matching degree with the entity names from a standard name library containing a plurality of standard entity names; Determining similarities between the entity names and the candidate entity names in at least one preset dimension; Synthesizing the similarity of the entity names and the candidate entity names in at least one preset dimension, and determining the candidate entity name with the highest comprehensive similarity of the entity name in at least one preset dimension from the plurality of candidate entity names; And determining the candidate entity name with the highest comprehensive similarity as a standard entity name corresponding to the entity name. According to the scheme, the applicable situation of entity name matching can be increased, the accuracyof entity name matching is improved, and manpower resource consumption is reduced.
