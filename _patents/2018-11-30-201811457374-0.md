核心方法
-----
根据生产设备的运行参数，使用机器学习算法预测发生各类异常的概率。  

摘要
-----
本发明公开了一种预警方法及装置，获取设备的预定运行参数在最近的预设历史时长内的若干测量值，并提取该若干测量值的多个维度的特征；将所提取的多个维度的特征输入与上述预定运行参数对应的预先训练好的预警模型，得到表征设备的上述预定运行参数在未来是否发生异常预警结果，实现了对设备的预定运行参数在未来是否发生异常的预测。    

Abstract
----
The embodiment of the invention discloses an early warning method and device. The method comprises the following steps that: obtaining a plurality of measurement values of the preset operation parameters of equipment in latest preset historical duration, and extracting the characteristics of a plurality of dimensions of the plurality of measurement values; and inputting the extracted characteristics of the plurality of dimensions into a pre-trained early warning model corresponding to the above preset operation parameters to obtain an early warning result which represents whether the above preset operation parameters of equipment are exceptional or not. Therefore, whether the preset operation parameters of equipment are exceptional or not in the future is predicted.
