核心方法
-----
在生产设备上，通过传递熵、相关性等方法，寻找影响收率的工艺参数，并通过随机森林预测产量。  

摘要
-----
本公开提供了一种构造产率预测模型的方法，包括获得训练数据，所述训练数据包括可能影响产率的多个因素的数据以及产率的数据，获得多个因素集以及与所述因素集对应的权重，基于因素与产率的相互关系，将所述多个因素映射到所述多个因素集中，以及基于处理后的训练数据和所述权重构造用于产率预测的随机森林模型，其中，所述权重与生成节点时，作为选择对应的因素集的因素的概率相关。本公开还提供了一种产率预测方法、一种构造产率预测模型的系统以及产率预测系统。    

Abstract
----
The present invention provides a method for constructing a yield prediction model. The method for constructing the yield prediction model includes the following steps that: training data are obtained,wherein the training data include the data of a plurality of factors that may affect yields, and the data of the yields; a plurality of factor sets and weights corresponding to the factor sets are obtained; the plurality of factors are mapped to the plurality of factor sets based on correlations between the factors and the yields; and a random forest model for yield prediction is constructed based on the processed training data and the weights, wherein the weights are related to probabilities of selecting the factors of the corresponding factor sets when nodes are generated. The present invention also provides a yield prediction method, a system for constructing a yield prediction model, and a yield prediction system.
