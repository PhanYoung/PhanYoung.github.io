核心方法
-----
根据当前车辆位置和能源状态，使用机器学习算法预测到达目的地时的能源状态，消除用户里程焦虑。  

摘要
-----
本发明提供了一种信息处理方法，应用于具有电驱动功能的车辆，所述方法包括：获取车辆位置信息、目的地信息以及可行驶里程的关联参数；根据所述车辆位置信息和所述可行驶里程的关联参数确定车辆是否可到达目的地或最近的充电地点；以及在可到达时提示乘员可到达信息。本公开还提供了一种具有电驱动功能的车辆。    

Abstract
----
The invention provides an information processing method applied to a vehicle with an electric driving function. The method comprises the steps of acquiring vehicle position information, destination information and associated parameters of driving mileage, determining whether the vehicle can reach a destination or the nearest charging place or not according to the vehicle position information and the associated parameters of the driving mileage, and prompting passengers of reaching information when the vehicle can arrive. The invention further provides the vehicle with the electric driving function.
