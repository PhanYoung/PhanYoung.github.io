核心方法
-----
将一系列化学反应抽象成图模型，寻找到达指定结点后成本最低、能耗最小、副产品价值最高等路径。  

摘要
-----
本发明实施例公开了化学反应路径获取方法、装置、电子设备及存储介质，预先构建化学反应关系有向图，利用化学反应关系有向图建立了多个化学反应的关联关系，即以图的形式将孤立的多个化学反应关联起来；目标反应物对象包括的至少一个反应物对应化学反应关系有向图中相应位置的节点，目标产物对象包括的至少一个产物对应化学反应关系有向图中相应位置的节点。本发明实施例目标化学反应路径的查询转换成化学反应关系有向图中至少两个节点之间路径的查询，即利用该化学反应关系有向图，可以得到目标化学反应路径；即使目标反应物对象与目标产物对象不属于同一化学反应方程，通过化学反应关系有向图，也可以得到目标化学反应路径。    

Abstract
----
The embodiments of the invention disclose a chemical reaction path acquisition method and device, an electronic device, and a storage medium. A chemical reaction relationship direct graph is constructed in advance, and a plurality of chemical reaction correlation relationships are established by the chemical reaction relationship directed graph, namely, isolated chemical reactions are associated in the form of a graph. A target reactant object comprises at least one reactant corresponding to a node at a corresponding position in the directed graph of the chemical reaction relationship, and a target product object comprises at least one product corresponding to a node at a corresponding position in the directed graph of the chemical reaction relationship. A target chemical reaction path query in that embodiment of the present invention is convert into a path query between at least two nodes in a chemical reaction relation digraph, namely, the target chemical reaction path can be obtainby using the chemical reaction relation digraph; and the target chemical reaction path is converted into a path query between at least two nodes in the chemical reaction relation digraph. Even if thetarget reactant object and the target product object do not belong to the same chemical reaction equation, the target chemical reaction path can be obtained through the directed graph of chemical reaction relationship.
