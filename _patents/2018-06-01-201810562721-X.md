核心方法
-----
通过双目相机，获取物体表面的深度信息，结合图像平面特征，通过卷积神经网络判断表面伤痕的类型和程度。  

摘要
-----
本公开提供了一种物体表面缺陷检测方法，包括：获取物体表面的图像的指定信息，所述指定信息包括：图像深度信息；基于所述图像深度信息，分析所述物体表面是否存在缺陷。本公开还提供了一种物体表面缺陷检测装置、一种实现上述物体表面缺陷检测方法的计算机设备。    

Abstract
----
A method for detecting a surface defect of an object is provided. That method includes: obtaining designation information of an image of an object surface, wherein the designation information includesimage depth information; and analyzing whether the object surface has a defect or not on the basis of the image depth information. The present disclosure also provides a device for detecting a surface detect of an object, and a computer device for realizing the method detecting the surface detect of the object.
